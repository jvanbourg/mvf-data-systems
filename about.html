The person who answers the phone

Marc Fertout

Founder & Principal Consultant

Marc holds bachelor's and advanced degrees in software engineering with a specialization in medical informatics — a field where sloppy data isn't an inconvenience, it's a liability. He built his career designing software architecture, fixing broken IT systems, and engineering databases for organizations where the numbers had to be right.

Over two decades in Europe, that work ranged from overhauling a national drug database to consolidating hospital data for the French Ministry of Health — projects whose data architecture helped secure more than $35 million in government funding.

Now based in Los Angeles, Marc brings that same discipline to small and mid-sized businesses: cleaning up back offices, untangling systems, and turning messy data into something you can actually run a company on.
