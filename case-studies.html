Case Studies

Explore a selection of projects that showcase our expertise and the successful outcomes we've delivered for our healthcare clients.

National Scale

Grand Duchy of Luxembourg — National Public Health System

Overhaul of the national drug database. Designed a comprehensive database architecture and API interface, enabling healthcare professionals to access real-time, patient-oriented data on a national scale.

Data Consolidation

French Ministry of Health

Sourced and consolidated disorganized, disaggregated data from the databases of three large hospitals to analyze the efficiency of expensive cancer treatments and improve outcomes at reduced cost.

€22M Funded

Bourgogne-Franche-Comté Regional Hospital System — e-Health

Supervised and modeled database architecture for regional e-Health projects, including heart failure, allergy monitoring, oncology, geriatrics, and Alzheimer's, with a total funding of 22 million euros from the national government of France for design and implementation.

€8.4M Funded

Grand Est Regional Hospital System — Telemedicine

Advised and developed a data-driven program for medical deserts (areas without sufficient medical coverage), resulting in the deployment of a telemedicine network for isolated individuals and securing a grant of 8.4 million euros from the national government of France.

€1.2M Funded

Bourgogne-Franche-Comté — Stroke Recurrence Prevention

Provided data management consulting for an innovative stroke recurrence prevention protocol, resulting in a successful project grant of 1.2 million euros from the national government of France for implementation.

€1M Funded

Bourgogne-Franche-Comté Regional Obesity Clinic

Provided data management consulting for an innovative care protocol in psycho-medical monitoring of obesity, leading to a successful national project grant of 1 million euros for implementation.
