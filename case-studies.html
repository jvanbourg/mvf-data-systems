Track Record

Proof, not promises

Before Los Angeles, Marc spent decades fixing data and systems for some of the most demanding clients there are: national health systems and government ministries in Europe. If it worked at that scale, it will work for your business.

National Scale

Overhaul of the national drug database. Designed a comprehensive database architecture and API interface, enabling healthcare professionals to access real-time, patient-oriented data on a national scale.

Data Consolidation

French Ministry of Health

Sourced and consolidated disorganized, disaggregated data from the databases of three large hospitals to analyze the efficiency of expensive cancer treatments and improve outcomes at reduced cost.
